Luyu Du is a Ph.D. Candidate in Public Administration and Policy at ASU School of Public Affairs and Graduate Student Fellow at the Center for Organization Research and Design (CORD). Her research interest broadly lies in science policy and public value science/societal impacts of research, focusing on co-production of knowledge and public organizations. It is a topic that she will be able to explore within the framework of STITCH.
